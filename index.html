💰 Iceland Money

🇮🇸
ICELAND KRÓNA (ISK)
1,000 kr = $7.35 USD
1,000 kr = £5.88 GBP
🧠 Easy way to think about it:
100 kr ≈ 73¢ (less than a dollar!)
1,000 kr ≈ $7 USD / £6 GBP
10,000 kr ≈ $74 USD / £59 GBP
Everything in Iceland looks expensive — it's just big numbers! 😅
💳 Good to know
Cards accepted everywhere
No need for cash usually
Tipping not expected 🙌
Iceland is one of the most cashless countries in the world!
